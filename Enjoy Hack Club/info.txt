SAMD21E18 based development board, with Enjoy Hack Club design. Alligator clip pads.

Easy-solder 0805 handsolder footprints, TQFP32. USB-C connector(may be tricky to solder).

Designed with KiCAD EDA. Fully Open Source, all source files provided.

Use Adafruit's guide on flashing bootloaders: https://learn.adafruit.com/how-to-program-samd-bootloaders

Supports CircuitPython.
